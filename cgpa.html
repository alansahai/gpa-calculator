Understanding CGPA

Learn about Cumulative Grade Point Average calculation

What is CGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is the average of all SGPAs earned by a student over all semesters. It provides an overall measure of academic performance throughout the entire course duration.

CGPA = (Sum of all SGPAs) ÷ (Number of Semesters)

CGPA Grading Scale

Outstanding 9.0 - 10.0
Excellent 8.0 - 8.9
Very Good 7.0 - 7.9
Good 6.0 - 6.9
Average 5.0 - 5.9
