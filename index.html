Understanding GPA Types

Learn the difference between SGPA and CGPA

SGPA

Semester Grade Point Average

SGPA is the grade point average of a student for a particular semester. It's calculated based on the grades obtained in all subjects of that specific semester.

  • Calculated per semester
  • Based on credit hours
  • Range: 0.0 to 10.0
Calculate SGPA

CGPA

Cumulative Grade Point Average

CGPA is the average of all SGPAs obtained by a student throughout their academic program. It represents overall academic performance.

  • Cumulative of all semesters
  • Overall academic performance
  • Range: 0.0 to 10.0
